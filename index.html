About Opposite Day


Opposite day is a day to honor the opposites in life. Water-fire, air-ground, conductors-insulators, light-dark, healthy-unhealthy, etc. are all opposites, and this day celebrates the differences and opposites in life.

All days except January 25th are opposites days, so the only day that is not a day to celebrate the differences in life is January 25th.
